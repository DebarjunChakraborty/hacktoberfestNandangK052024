Hacktoberfest 2024

A Celebration of Open Source Contribution!

What is Hacktoberfest?

Hacktoberfest is an annual event in October that encourages developers to contribute to open-source projects. It is open to everyone, from seasoned developers to beginners. Participants can earn exclusive prizes and a sense of accomplishment by making meaningful contributions to the open-source community.

How to Participate?

  • Register on the Hacktoberfest website.
  • Find open-source repositories to contribute to.
  • Make pull requests to repositories on GitHub.
  • Submit at least 4 pull requests during October to complete the challenge.

Benefits of Participating

  • Support the open-source community.
  • Gain experience in collaborative coding.
  • Win cool swag (T-shirts, stickers, etc.).
  • Learn new technologies and improve your skills.
